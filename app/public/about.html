Why Pedal Pals

Pedal Pals is more than an app, it is a movement. We encourage integrity, kindness, equality, confidence, and respect during all stages of the freindship. Pedal Pals is where cyclist go to learn how to make meaningful connections based on personality and a common hobby.

Let's talk product

Pedal Pals was first founded to challenge the antiquated rules of fiding people to cycle with. Now, Pedal Pals empowers users to connect with confidence when meeting cycling friends online. We prioritize kindness and respect, providing a safe online community for users to build new relationships.
